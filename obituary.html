Obituary 

MaOtang Prisca passed on peaceful from this life in the month of September 2021 after a gradual decline in health. She was surrounded and supported by her loving family and caregivers during the last days of her life. MaOtang Prisca was born in Mamfe town to Chief Asuagbor Jerome and Ma Angela Tabe. She finally settled at Nchang village most of her life. After her kids started growing up, she decided to commit herself to take care of her mother at Nchang village for the rest of the time before her mother passed away.
MaOtang is survived by three children, three grandchildren.
She is also survived by her brothers and sisters. She was also preceded in death by her brother Mr. Phillip Asuagbor.
A funeral service would be held in Mamfe.
